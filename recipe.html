Chamallow
S'mores

Yields: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 HARIBO© Chamallows
  • 1 Hershey© Chocolate Bar
  • 8 Digestive Crackers

Prepare

1. Break off a row of the Hershey© bar and place on top of one cracker.
2. Roast your chamallow over the bonfire to your preferred "doneness".
3. With the chamallow still on the roasting stick, rest it on top of the chocolate. Put the other cracker on top and pull the stick out, gently squeezing the crackers together to hold everything in place.
4. Enjoy!
